Other Payment Options

Below are the other possible payent options that MyServicer Can Process

Auto Debit

Never miss a payment. When you sign up for auto debit, your payment is automatically deducted from the bank account of your choosing. Plus, you could even save on your interest rate* while your account is in an active repayment status!

Via Mail

Mail your payment using the address located on your statement. Make sure that you include your MyServicer account number, including the “D, J, or E”, so that your payment is processed in a timely manner and correctly applied to your accounts.

Bank Online Pay

Use your bank's online bill pay service (make sure they don't charge a fee). You'll just need to give them the Payee (MyServicer), your account number, and the payment address, which is the P.O. box address on the bottom of your statement.

Via Phone

Call us at 888.486.4722 and make a payment through our automated phone system or with a Customer Service Representative.
