Badges

What is a badge? A badge is a mark of achievement within the MyServicer world. Badges are given as rewards for completing tasks or achieving important goals and milestones. The badges are to help users easily see how they are doing in addition to giving them a sense of personal achievement as they earn badges.

Not all badges are created equal, and some will be harder to achieve. Badges come in 4 colors:

  • Black - Basic
  • Bronze - Level 1
  • Silver - Level 2
  • Gold - Level 3
